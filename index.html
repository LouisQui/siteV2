    FAQ

    "How do you avoid bots ?"

    - Partners can setup a minimum of followers for getting rewards
    - Tweet2Earn is limited to 1 tweet/month/user by projects
    - Each user needs to link their wallet w/ twitter on our website
    - The team checkup every day all the tweets to make sure there is no bots

    "How do we become partner ?"

    We select partners by ourselves, if you want to use The Incentive send us a message on Twitter. If your project is selected, we will give you access to the configuration page !

    "What token should I use ?"

    You could reward users with USDC or with your own token.

    "Can I link a burn wallet ?"

    YES ! We recommend using a burn wallet, connecting with Solana is just used to replace email/password and we don't use your wallet, for remunerate users we ask you to send funds to a wallet under our control.
