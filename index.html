    ROADMAP

    • Q1-2023

      Rebranding

      Creation of a new branding with a French designer, new logo, new colors...

      Bot discord

      Creation and implementation of a Discord bot to warn your community when you add a new raid.

      Giveaway

      Each week, we will offer the NFT of one of our customers who has been most activite on the week for Tweet2Earn.

    • Q1-2023

      Bot Twitter

      Reinforcement of our anti-bot twitter to propose a Tweet2Earn with only real users.

      Creation of filters

      Set up filters, with the total budget, and a campaign start and end date. Setting up a bigger or smaller reward, depending on the engagement of the tweets.

    • Q1-2023

      Creation of the NFT

      Creation and mint of the NFT, The Incentive that gives access to different advantages and things that we will reveal soon.

      Launching on Ethereum

      The Incentive and the Tweet2Earn land on a new blockchain and became open to thousands of new projects.

      Creation of the token

      Placement of the token on the markets, and distribution to the owner of the NFT. Rewarding of tweets in $INCENTIVE.

    • Q1-2023

      Creation of the coworking metaverse

      Creation of a coworking platform in the metaverse, with parties, speakers, and offices for the NFT holders.

      Filter evolution

      Implementation of more filters for our tool, you will be able to ask your customers to put links, photos, keywords, and customize as much as possible your needs in their tweets to create the best community marketing tool ever.

    • Q1-2023

      Launching on Tezos

      The Incentive launching on a new blockchain opens up thousands of new projects by preempting available in the Tezos blockchain.

      Finishing of all filters

      The Incentive becomes the most complete tool, you can filter your customers by language, location, and interest to fit with campaign needed of the moment. You will be able to run multiple campaigns with different needs to grow and go to the moon.

    • Q1-2023

      The Incentive becomes decentralized

      Implementation of everything under smart contract. And implementation of the governance under DAO.

      The Incentive lands on other social networks

      Implementation of the tool on Instagram, Facebook, Discord, and other social networks requested by our customers

    • Q1-2023

      Open to Web2

      Twitter users will be able to connect to the site with an email address and earn money without creating a wallet. This opens The Incentive to Web2 companies and Web2 Twitter users.
